About Us:




Our cakes are handmade with natural ingredients, and whilst we are ordinary people, we want to create extraordinary things. We want to play our part to bring people back into local communities. We can’t imagine doing anything more gratifying than baking our, and your, cakes.

Sara Blackwood (CEO) about Delicious Cakes

We put love and compassion in the process. We pray for the food to be beneficial for the people who eat it. And then when they go to the kitchen, same thing, it's a mindful exercise, it's a meditation.

Ian Morison (Pastry Chef) about Delicious Cakes

Delicious Cakes was formed as a partnership between Sara and Ian to make top quality cakes for the existing bakeries. The success of the product led to the opening of six bakeries and more than 150 wholesale accounts. The product range was gradually expanded to include very extraordinary cakes and savouries which share our bread philosophy.
